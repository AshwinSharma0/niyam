Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Niyam

Is Niyam free to use?

Yes, Niyam is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to tools that help build better habits.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to get started. Your data is stored locally on your device using secure browser storage.

Will my data sync across devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on your device. We're working on a cloud sync feature that will be available soon.

Can I track multiple habits at once?

Absolutely! You can track as many habits as you want, and organize them by categories for better management.
